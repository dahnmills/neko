Goals
Unleash creativity by generating a wide variety of ideas or solutions in 8 minutes.
Preparation:
No specific preparation needed
Tools:
  • A4 sheets
  • Pencils and eraser
  • Colored markers
  • Stickers (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Each participant fold their piece of paper in 8
  2. Set the timer for eight minutes
  3. Participants sketch roughly in silence, 1 idea per minute
  4. At the end of the first round, each participant share their ideas during 3-minutes
  5. You can repeat another few rounds
People needed:
1 facilitator for 2 to 8 participants
Duration:
1h to 1h30
Difficulty:
1/5
Tips:
Each participant get 3 to 5 stickers to vote for their favorite ideas.
